BIO GALLERY TOUR

  • Joe Occhiuti

    A recent addition to the band, Joe has provided keyboard, bass, and
    backing vocals since 2019. Joe is fulfilling the position after prior
    bassist, Justin Morrow, left the band to join Motionless in White.

  • Spencer Charnas

    Original founder of the band in 2002, Spencer is Ice Nine Kills' main
    vocalist and songwriter.

  • Justin DeBlieck

    Currently on hiatus, Justin provides lead guitar, co-lead vocals, piano,
    and production value to the band. He has been with them since 2009.

  • Patrick Galante

    Patrick, formerly of the band Affiance, has been with Ice Nine Kills
    since 2018, and provides drums and percussion. He also plays
    drums in American Nosebleed.

  • Ricky Armellino

    Ricky provides guitar and back-up vocals in Ice nine Kills, and has been
    with them since 2018. Ricky is also a part of the band HAWK, and
    provides vocals for MouthData as well.

  • Dan Sugarman

    Filling in for Justin DeBlieck, Dan provides lead guitar for Ice Nine Kills.
    He has been with the band since 2019.

  • Last Chance to Make Amends

    2006

    Last Chance to Make Amends was Ice Nine Kills' debut independent
    album, released on April 20, 2006. Initially 10 songs long (later two
    hidden tracks were added on a re-release) this album features a mixture
    of alt-rock, pop-punk, and ska, consistent with the band's early sound.

  • The Burning

    2007

    Released through Red Blue Records, The Burning first arrived to the
    public on November 20, 2007. Following this, the band played a
    supporting role on several national tours. They then released acoustic
    versions of two songs off the album in 2009.

  • Safe is Just a Shadow

    2010

    Released on July 12, 2010, their second studio album won them
    attention from Heavy Metal Magazine Revolver, as well as a two-week
    supporting spot on Vans Warped Tour 2010. They then went on the play
    supporting roles in several tours across the US. A 10-song album, Ice Nine
    Kills re-recorded and re-released Safe is Just a Shadow in 2017, citing
    their development of skills and sound as a reason for renewal.

  • The Predator Becomes the Prey

    2014

    Released on January 21, 2014, The Predator Becomes the Prey debuted
    at #3 on the top heatseaker's chart. Ice Nine Kills went on to perform
    on the Monster Energy stage for the full run of Vans Warped Tour 2014.
    In April, Ice Nine Kills started on one of their first headlining tours.

  • Every Trick in The Book

    2015

    Now signed to Fearless Records, their current label, Ice Nine Kills
    released Every Trick in The Book on December 4, 2015. The first of their
    albums that exclusively feature songs based on a specific subject, this
    album focuses on novels and other stories.

  • The Silver Scream

    2018

    Their latest album, Ice Nine Kills released the Silver Scream—featuring
    songs based on horror movies—on October 5, 2018, their highest charting
    debut yet. With 13 songs, they pay homage to the greatest of classic and
    modern horror. On October 25, 2019, Ice Nine Kills Released The Silver
    Scream: Final Cut, an extended cut of the album featuring 2 new songs,
    as well as several acoustic versions of the songs.
